Are We Heading Toward A Recession?

Ever since the 2008 crisis, Economies around the globe had been adopting Quantitative Easing to recover from the traumas of the event. However, it is not hard to see that we're yet again approaching the same point in the economic cycle, where Interest rates are highest it's ever been since 2009, Consumer Price Index (CPI) is outrunning wage-rises, and Housing has grown to the point it's unimaginable to afford for many average Australian households. While the Economy grasps to stay above the water, it is hard to say which part of the economic machine will first give-in.
